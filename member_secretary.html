Secretary


Israel Johnson


Israel Johnson is a software engineer at the Naval Surface Warfare Center, Dahlgren Division, concentrating in projects related to machine learning and artificial intelligence. He has a Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Virginia, where his main concentrations were in biomedical engineering and signals processing.

He plans to pursue graduate school in the field of computer science, with keen interests in machine learning and image processing. One of his long-term research goals is to find new and different techniques in extracting relevant data from image datasets, and study how those techniques change over time.
